About

Engineer by craft,
artist by nature.

I'm a full-stack engineer with deep roots in Angular, from version 2 through to v21. I don't just build UIs; I architect the systems that make them scalable, maintainable, and fast.

At Markt-Pilot, I built the entire microfrontend infrastructure from scratch and was the engineer who introduced Angular Signals, leading the migration, establishing the patterns, and upskilling the team.

I care about the details: performance, accessibility, design consistency, and reducing friction in everyday workflows. I use AI-assisted tools, Claude Code and GitHub Copilot, as a genuine part of how I ship.

When I'm not engineering, I paint. Art keeps my eye sharp for design and my brain thinking visually, which makes me a better frontend engineer.
